I am a Ph.D. Candidate and Graduate Research Assistant at the University of California, Merced, working in the Forest Ecohydrology & Watershed Systems (FEWS) Lab under the supervision of Professor Safeeq Khan and Professor Joshua Viers. My expertise lies in integrated hydrological modeling, climate change impacts on water systems, and sustainable groundwater management in arid and semi-arid regions.

Educational Background

  • PhD (2022–present), Environmental Systems, UC Merced
  • PhD Coursework (2022), Water Resources Engineering & Management, Bahir Dar University
  • MS (2018), Hydraulic and Water Resources Engineering, Bahir Dar University
  • BS (2014), Water Resources and Irrigation Management, Wollega University

Previous Experience

  • University of Gondar (2016–2022): Assistant Lecturer & Lecturer
  • Dilla University (2015–2016): Assistant Lecturer
