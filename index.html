Welcome

PhD Candidate & Graduate Research Assistant at the University of California, Merced, working in the Forest Ecohydrology & Watershed Systems (FEWS) Lab under the supervision of Professor Safeeq Khan and Professor Joshua Viers. I am a passionate hydrologist and environmental systems researcher committed to work on hydrological dynamics in climate-stressed regions.

My research focuses on modeling regional surface water and groundwater systems under a changing climate. I utilize advanced numerical and analytical methods to analyze complex hydrological systems and contribute to innovative, data-driven solutions for a water-secure future.
