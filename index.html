Welcome

I am a Ph.D. Candidate and Graduate Research Assistant at the University of California, Merced, working in the Forest Ecohydrology & Watershed Systems (FEWS) Lab under the supervision of Professor Safeeq Khan and Professor Joshua Viers. I am a passionate hydrologist and environmental systems researcher committed to developing sustainable water management strategies for climate-stressed regions.

My research focuses on modeling surface water and groundwater systems under a changing climate. I utilize analytical and numerical methods to analyze complex hydrological systems and contribute to innovative, data-driven solutions for a water-secure future.
