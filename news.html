Latest Updates

  • Exciting News: Review Paper Accepted! I'm pleased to announce that our review paper, "Global advances in managed aquifer recharge: a systematic synthesis", has been accepted for publication in Environmental Reviews. This work, led by Berhanu G. Sinshaw and co-authored by Prof. Helen E. Dahlke, Prof. Joshua H. Viers, and prof. Mohammad Safeeq, presents a comprehensive global analysis of managed aquifer recharge (MAR) strategies over the past four decades. It highlights key innovations, challenges, and opportunities in addressing groundwater sustainability amid growing climate and water security pressures.
