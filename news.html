  • (2025) Exciting News: Paper Accepted!

    I'm thrilled to share that our review paper, "Global advances in managed aquifer recharge: a systematic synthesis", has been accepted for publication in Environmental Reviews. This work, led by Berhanu G. Sinshaw and co-authored by Prof. Helen E. Dahlke, Prof. Joshua H. Viers, and Prof. Safeeq Khan, offers a comprehensive global synthesis of managed aquifer recharge (MAR) strategies spanning the last four decades. The paper highlights key innovations, challenges, and opportunities in advancing groundwater sustainability in the face of increasing climate and water security concerns.
